The Journey

From IKAG to Tradorr

A story of innovation and community empowerment

December 2024

The Idea

Our founder experienced firsthand the frustration of searching for trusted service providers through word-of-mouth recommendations. This inspired the creation of a platform that would bring structure and visibility to the underutilized talent in the informal sector.

January 2025

I Know A Guy (IKAG)

The platform was born from a simple yet persistent problem in many African cities: finding reliable and affordable service professionals quickly and safely. Initially named "I Know A Guy" (IKAG), the service began connecting customers with vetted providers in Kampala.

March 2025

Growing Community

Within months, IKAG had built a network of over 50 service providers and served hundreds of customers across Uganda. The platform became known for its commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

April 2025

Becoming Tradorr

Formerly known as IKAG (I Know A Guy), we rebranded to Tradorr to better reflect our mission â€” connecting trust, trade, and tradition across essential services in Africa and beyond.

Today

Expansion Vision

Today, Tradorr is expanding across East Africa with a vision to become a leading platform for service providers across Africa. We continue to innovate and improve our technology while staying true to our core mission of empowering service providers and delighting customers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Tradorr

How do I become a service provider on Tradorr?

To become a service provider, sign up on our platform, complete your profile with all required information and verification documents, and submit for approval. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 48 hours.

How does Tradorr ensure the quality of service providers?

We have a rigorous verification process that includes document verification, skills assessment, and background checks. Additionally, our review system allows customers to rate service providers, ensuring accountability and quality service.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with a service?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with a service, please contact our customer support team within 48 hours of service completion. We'll work with you and the service provider to resolve the issue or provide an appropriate solution.

In which areas does Tradorr operate?

Currently, Tradorr operates in major cities across Uganda, with plans to expand to other East African countries soon. Check the location search feature to see if we're available in your area.

How do I pay for services on Tradorr?

Currently, payments are made directly to service providers after the service is completed. We're working on implementing an in-app payment system to make transactions even more convenient and secure.

Still have questions?

We're here to help. Contact our support team or check our comprehensive FAQ page.
